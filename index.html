Janet Van Dyne

The Wasp

  • Codename: Wasp
  • Powers: Flight, Sting
  • Civilian Job: Fashion Designer
  • Status: Unknown

Janet van Dyne is the wife of Hank Pym and the mother of Hope van Dyne. She operated by the codename Wasp in S.H.I.E.L.D. until she sacrificed herself to disarm a Soviet ICBM bound to the United States in 1987, saving millions of lives but also becoming lost to the Quantum Realm.

Janet van Dyne married Hank Pym, a scientist who developed a way to shrink a human to the size of an ant and maintain their strength. He also discovered a way to communicate with ants. Together they had a daughter named Hope van Dyne. Ant-Man and the Wasp.

Van Dyne convinced Hank Pym to let her join his missions for S.H.I.E.L.D.. With her own shrinking suit, she became known as the Wasp alongside Pym as the Ant-Man.

Janet worked for S.H.I.E.L.D, but also pursued her dream of being a fashion designer. Ever since she was a small child, she would be enthralled with her mother's closet. She was known for styling many celebrities and kept her superhero activities private.
